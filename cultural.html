Cultural Events

Apart from being the thech fest of C& IT dept. We always promote and inculcate art and crafts.

Solo Singing

The event provides a platform to all thre budding singers to mesmerizethe audience. participants show off their talent and win exciting prizes. Let the singer rise in you, come join us!

  • The time allotted for each performance is 3 mins.
  • The participant will not be allowed to refer to the lyrics while singing.
  • The performance will be judged on the basis of voice quality, song selection, pitch, temp and overall presentation.
  • The performers should bring own instruments if required for the performance.
  • Participants can get their own track or can perform with one accompanist.
  • REGISTRATION: Rs 50.
  • Prize :Rs 500.

    • Rangoli

    Rangoli is an art originated from the Indian subcontinent, in which patterns are created on courtyards using fabulous colours. Show of ur skills using this colours and make an mind blowing rangoli and impress the judges and win huge prize amount.

  • Students can register as a team of 2 or 3
  • The time limit is 1 hour 30 mins.
  • Participants have to get their own supplies.
  • The judges decision is final.
  • REGISTRATION: Rs 100.
  • Prize :Rs 1000.
