Counter Strike

It is a series of multi-player first person shooter video games, in which teams of terrorists battle to perpetrate an act of terror and counter terrorists try to prevent it.

    Each team can have max of 5 participants.
    Time limit for each round will be 3 mins
    The game will be installd by the coordinators before the start
    Using cheats or mods will lead to disqualification

Need For Speed

The series centers around illicit street racing and in general tasks players to complete various types of races while evading the local law enforcement in police pursuits.

    The no of participants for a race is limited to 4.
    Only sprint races are conducted
    Only stock cars can be used
    The game will be installed by the coordinators before the start
    Using cheats or mods will lead to disqualification
