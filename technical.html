Technical JAM

  • Participants will participate solo
  • The jam master will give a topic to the group of participants
  • All the participants have to speak on the same topic for 60 seconds but not at the same time.
  • The jam master decides who starts. The person who starts should talk without any grammatical mistakes , long pauses, deviation from topic and any other point the jam master decides.
  • In case he does any of those then any other participant can point it out and start talking
  • The same rules are applicable for him also
  • Scoring is done based on number of seconds you speak and also based on who ends the minute
  • Jam masters call will be final.
